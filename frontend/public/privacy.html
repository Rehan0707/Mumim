Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 12, 2026

Munim.ai helps shop owners manage customer conversations, inventory, orders, payments, and analytics. This policy explains what data the service handles and how it is protected.

Data We Process

We may process shop profile details, WhatsApp numbers, customer names, chat messages, media references, product catalogs, order history, payment references, and operational analytics.

How Data Is Used

Data is used to answer customer queries, create and manage orders, update inventory, generate payment links, show dashboard analytics, detect service issues, and improve language and search quality.

Sharing

We share data only with service providers needed to run the product, such as WhatsApp messaging, payment, hosting, database, logging, and AI infrastructure providers. We do not sell customer data.

Retention And Deletion

Shop owners can request export or deletion of their business data by contacting support. Some records may be retained where required for security, fraud prevention, accounting, or legal compliance.

Security

Production deployments should use authenticated dashboard sessions, signed webhooks, encrypted transport, restricted database access, and separate production secrets.

Contact

For privacy questions, contact privacy@munim.ai.
